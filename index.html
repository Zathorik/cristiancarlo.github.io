About Me

Detailed oriented, performance driven and highly accomplished Industrial Designer with 6+ years of experience developing innovative product designs.

Adept at completing projects within budget and in a timely manner. Excellent knowledge of multiple design software programs, great teamwork abilities, and extraordinary communication skills.

Proficient in computer-aided design and drafting software, three-dimensional design and various manufacturing methods. Successful in working long hours in challenging environments.

Able to identify market trends inspire and satisfy customer’s demands, define and implement product design process, troubleshoot and solve problems following design thinking methodologies.

Packaging, end-user and out-of-box experience engineering background, former AA engineer at Poly, responsible for global enterprise and retail primary, secondary and tertiary packaging for a wide range of premium audio and video products.

Currently taking Google’s UX Design certificate, transitioning to UX to become the Swiss army knife of design that can tackle multiple design problems, forecast a new future, shift conventional thinking and behaviors within the digital world.
