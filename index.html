About Me.

I'm a dynamic and versatile designer, with a passion for crafting meaningful user experiences that bridge the gap between form and function. With a background in industrial design and expertise in packaging design engineering, I am excited to bring a unique perspective to the world of UX product design.

Throughout my journey as an industrial designer, I've had the privilege of immersing myself in the world of product aesthetics, ergonomics, and manufacturing processes. I've honed the ability to transform concepts into tangible, user-centric designs that not only capture attention but also seamlessly integrate into users' lives. This experience has developed in me a keen eye for detail and an understanding of the delicate balance between aesthetics and usability.
